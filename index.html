Ayuda

"Giving ayuda is a vital act of support for communities facing hardship. It provides essential resources such as food, financial assistance, and basic necessities to families in need, helping them navigate through challenging situations."

First Friday Mass

Our First Friday Mass is a special devotion held on the first Friday of each month, dedicated to the Sacred Heart of Jesus. It provides an opportunity for graces and blessings through devotion to the Sacred Heart.

Sunday Mass

Sunday Mass brings the parish community together to celebrate the Lord's Day. It is a time for communal prayer, reflection, and Eucharistic celebration.

About Us

Our Mission

Sto. Niño Catholic School was founded with the mission to provide a faith-based education that fosters spiritual growth and academic excellence.

Our Vision

We believe in nurturing the whole child—mind, body, and spirit—through a curriculum that integrates strong Catholic values with rigorous academics.

Our Values

Through service, prayer, and academic challenge, our students grow to become individuals of character, responsibility, and leadership.
